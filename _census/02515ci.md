text: 'GENERAL INFORMATION  Enumeration Districts In most cases, the Enumeration Districts
  (ED) of Lake County, Colorado Federal Census represent a specific precinct. However,  some
  contain more than one precinct.  Precincts represented by each ED are as follows:  ED
  72, Pct. 1 ED 79, Pct. 8  ED 73, Pct. 2 ED 80, Pct. 9  ED 74, Pct. 3 ED 81, Pcts.
  10, 12, 13 ED 75, Pct. 4 ED 82, Pct. 11  ED 76, Pct. 5 ED 83, Pct. 14, 15  ED 77,
  Pct. 6 ED 84, Pct. 16  ED 78, Pct. 7 Ed 85, Pct. 17  Each ED has numbered sheets.
  The numbers appear in the upper right-hand corner of each sheet. Only the ED number
  and the Sheet Number are used in this index.     Supervisor District__ Sheet No.  Enumeration
  District “0c _ 2e ad  2 Ward of City     There are often other numbers and comments
  written in by the census taker.  Each census also has numbered lines. Usually the
  "A" pages run  from 1-50; the "B" pages run from 51-100. However, due to census
  taker error, the "A" and "B" numbering may be reversed.  Reading the All-Name Index  The
  all-name index contains the following (example):  ABBOTT, BLANCH X “7 S a name age
  sex sheet No. line No.  To find a name in the original census film, follow these
  steps:  1) Roll the film to the ED number needed  2) Find the correct "A" or "B"
  page  3) Find the name on the numbered line of the oa or soe page.    '
