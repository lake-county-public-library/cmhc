text: 'LEADVILLE STREET DIRECTORY.  EXPLANATION.  The system adopted for numbering
  is that of a separate hundred to each block. Streets crossing Harrison avenue are
  numbered respectively east and west, commencing with 100 at Harrison avenue, and
  increasing both east and west. Streets running parallel with Harrison avenue are
  numbered north and south from Chestnut.  Alder—Fourth east of Harrison avenue, from
  north to south limits.  Ash--Sixth east of Harrison avenue, from Third north to
  limits.  Chestnut—Crossing s6uth end of Harrison avenue, running from Oak to west
  limits.  Dexter—Seventh west of Harrison avenue, from north to south limits.  Eighth—Seventh
  north of Chestnut, from east to west limits.  Eleventh—Tenth north of Chestnut,
  from Harrison avenue east to limits.  Elm—First south of Chestnut, from east to
  west limits.  Fifth—Fourth north of Chestnut, from east to west limits.  Fourth—tThird
  north of Chestnut, from east to west limits.  Front—Second south of Chestnut, from
  east to west limits.  Fryer—Seventh east of Harrison avenue, from Third north to
  limits.  Harrison Avenue—Running northerly and southerly through center of city.  Hazel—Third
  east of Harrison avenue, from north to south limits. '
