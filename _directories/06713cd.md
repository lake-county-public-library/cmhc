text: 'LEADVILLE STREET DIRECTORY.  EXPLANATION.  The system adopted for numbering
  is that of a separate hundred to each block. Streets crossing Harrison avenue are
  numbered respectively east and west, commencing with 100 at Harrison avenue, and
  increasing both east and west. Streeta running parallel with Harrison avenue are
  numbered north and south from Chestnut.  Alder—Fourth east of Harrison avenue, from
  north to south limits.  Ash—Sixth east of Harrison avenue, from Third north to limits.  Chestnut—Crossing
  south end of Harrison avenue, running from Oak to west limits.  Dexter—Seventh west
  of Harrison avenue, from north to south limits.  Eighth—Seventh north of Chestnut,
  from east to west limits.  Eleventh—Tenth north of Chestnut, from Harrison avenue
  east to limits.  EJm—First south of Chestnut, from east to west limits.  Fifth—Fourth
  north of Chestnut, from east to west limits.  Fourth—Third north of Chestuut, from
  east to west limits.  Front—Second south of Chestnut, from east to west limits.  Fryer—Seventh
  east of Harrison avenue, from Third north to limits.  Harrison Avenue—Running northerly
  and southerly through center of city.  Hazel—Third east of Harrison avenue, from
  north to south limits.  Hemlock—Second east of Harrison avenue, from north to south
  limits.  Hickory—Third east of Harrison avenue, from Chestnut north to Third.  James—Fifth
  west of Harrison avenue, from north to south limits.  Leiter Avenue—Third west of
  Harrison avenue, from north to south limits. '
